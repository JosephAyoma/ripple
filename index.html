About Ripple-EDD

Ripple Education Drivers is a leading nonprofit initiative driving transformative education across East Africa. We are dedicated to fostering learner-centered education by championing models such as the Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC). Our core belief is that educational transformation begins with informed and engaged communities. We work collaboratively with parents, educators, policy influencers, and civil society to demystify education reforms, clarify the objectives of CBC, and provide platforms for dialogue, collaboration, and shared learning. By bridging knowledge gaps and fostering partnerships, we help build inclusive, future-ready education systems that support every learner's growth and potential.
